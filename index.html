New draft rules targeting in-game spending wipe billions from China’s tech giants

Hong Kong CNN — Shares of several Chinese tech giants tanked in Hong Kong on Friday, wiping billions off their collective value, ﻿after Beijing unveiled a new set of regulations to restrict online spending in the gaming industry as part of its wider crackdown.
